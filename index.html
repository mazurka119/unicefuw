Unicef at UW. |
We are the official RSO (Registered Student Organization) affiliated with the

United Nation's Children's Fund

at the University of Washington in Seattle.

“UNICEF works in over 190 countries and territories to save children’s lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfil their potential, from early childhood through adolescence.”



Here at UW, we strive to aid the Seattle community and are dedicated to improving children welfare through community service, education, fundraising, and advocacy.

If you're interested in making a difference, this is the place for you.

How do I join Unicef at UW?

It's completely free to join the RSO! Here's how you can get started.

• Become a member of the discord server and introduce yourself

• Attend the next virtual or in-person meeting

• Get involved with your community through fundraising or volunteer events hosted by the club

• If you're interested in leadership positions, apply to be an officer for upcoming quarters!
